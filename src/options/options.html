YouTube Interest Filter

Show only the videos you care about. Everything else is hidden.

Interest packs

Click a topic to add a ready-made set of keywords. Pick as many as you like — you can fine-tune the list below afterwards.

Your interests

One keyword or phrase per line or comma-separated. A video is kept if its title or channel matches any of these. Leave empty to show everything.

Advanced settings

Block words

One per line or comma-separated. A video matching any of these is always hidden, even if it matches an interest.

Always-show channels

One channel name per line or comma-separated. Every video from these channels is shown — even if its title doesn't match an interest. (A block word still hides it.)
